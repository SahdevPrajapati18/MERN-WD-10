About the Fest

Resonance is the most awaited annual music extravaganza of our College that brings together passionate performers and enthusiastic audiences under one roof. It's a two-day celebration of sound, rhythm, and harmony featuring genres from classical to EDM. Whether you’re a soloist, part of a band, or just love vibing with good music—Resonance has something for everyone.


Events Line-Up:

Day 1

  • Inaugural Ceremony - 10:00 AM
  • Solo Singing Competition - 11:00 AM
  • Battle of Bands (Rock/Indie/Fusion) - 2:00 PM
  • Acoustic Jam Session - 5:00 PM
  • DJ Night - 7:00 PM onwards

Day 2

  • Open Mic and Unplugged Performaces - 11:00 AM
  • Rap & Beatbox Showdown - 2:00 PM
  • Faculty Jam Session (Fun Segment) - 4:00 PM
  • Headliner Performance (Celebrity Artist)- 7:00 PM
  • Closing Ceremony & Awards - 9:30 PM
